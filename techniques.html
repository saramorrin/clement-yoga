Meditation at home

Try this simple meditation technique for the best start to your day

Roll out your mat.

Sit on the mat with palms together in front of your chest. Close your eyes.

Clear your mind of all thoughts and let the light flood in.
Slowly take a deep breath in, filling your lungs, gently breath out letting your chest relax.
Repeat this 8 times.

Forward fold gently onto your mat, palms together and arms outstretched.

Relax back onto the mat, palms open and facing upwards.
